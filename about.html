Hello there, thanks for using this tool!



First off, I wanna say that this is my first experience at making and programming. This is my first experience with programming even! For a long time, I have been wanting to explore this field, and see what it has to offer for me, and in turn, what I had to offer for others. That's why I decided to make this website. I felt like creating this tool would make it a lot easier for people to keep their progress sorted out (trust me, I was close to printing and coloring the maps myself). In need of such a tool, I decided that this was the time to start learning how to write code.


I explicitly didn't want to use any webpage development software, as I wanted to test the waters with actual programming from scratch. While a lot of errors and mistakes were made on the way, even if it seems like that'd be unlikely for such a simple website, I managed to create something that I am content with, and I hope that people would actually find worth it to use in their gameplay.


As for right now, I plan on updating this site every so often to include and change content that I still feel needs to be refined. If you want to give any feedback or suggestions for me to consider adding to the website, please click the "Feedback" text down below.


Thanks to several people, forums and online schools for helping me figure things out during my learning process, and thank you again for checking out this little project of mine. <3
